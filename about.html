My Story

I've spent my career at the intersection of technology and human communication. What started as managing web content for the British Foreign & Commonwealth Office evolved into something I never expected - building AI systems that help businesses automate their most challenging digital operations.

The Government Years

For eleven years, I managed digital campaigns across 30+ countries for the FCO. I trained ambassadors and high commissioners on digital strategy, developed content management systems that worked across cultures, and learned how to make complex technology feel human.

This wasn't just about social media posts. I was helping diplomatic missions navigate crisis communications, cultural sensitivities, and the challenge of reaching audiences who spoke different languages, used different platforms, and had completely different expectations.

The Realization

Most companies face the same challenges I solved in government - how do you create content that works across different markets? How do you maintain brand consistency while scaling internationally? How do you automate without losing the human touch?

The Pivot to AI

In 2019, I left the FCO to start my own consulting practice. I began helping startups and growing companies with their digital strategies, but I kept running into the same problem - they were drowning in content creation.

That's when I discovered AI wasn't just about chatbots and automation. It was about solving the exact problems I'd been working on for years, but at scale. So I got my IBM AI Developer certification and started building actual solutions.

What I'm Building Now

Today, I create AI-powered systems that generate content, optimize campaigns, and automate social media workflows. But unlike most AI consultants, I understand the strategic and cultural complexity behind these tools.

I'm currently developing AI automation tools for fractional executives - helping C-suite professionals scale their personal brands and thought leadership. It's the perfect combination of my government experience (high-stakes communication) and my AI expertise (scalable automation).
