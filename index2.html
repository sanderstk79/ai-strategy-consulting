The Thing About Strategy

Look, everyone thinks they understand strategy. But here's what I've learned after 12 years of working with CEOs who couldn't sleep and CFOs who were running out of answers—strategy isn't about perfect plans. It's about... well, it's about seeing what others miss.

I started at McKinsey (because of course I did), but the real education came later. Watching a $50M logistics company save itself by doing the exact opposite of what the market research said. Helping a tech startup become profitable by killing their most promising product line. That's when I realized—

The best strategies often feel wrong at first. Like swimming upstream when everyone else is floating with the current. But that's where the breakthrough happens. That's where billion-dollar moats get built.
